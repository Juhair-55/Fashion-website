Glasses Care Tips

  1. Always store glasses in a protective case when not in use
  2. Clean lenses with a microfiber cloth, never with paper towels
  3. Avoid placing glasses lens-down on surfaces
  4. Have lenses professionally adjusted every 6 months
  5. Keep glasses away from extreme heat
